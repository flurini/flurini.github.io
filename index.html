NUMERO UNO: go street performing (but strategically)

Do you have a talent(play an instrument, are funny, can chug a bottle of water in 0.2 seconds, etc.)? Then street performing would suit you perfectly! But you can't just turn it into your daily 9:00 to 17.00, that would be miserable! You gotta do it strategically.

STRATEGY 1 (yeah we got substeps now baby): Make it an EVENT

This way, your performance will stand out from the others, because it only happens once in a lifetime(or a year, month, however you want it). You could start by putting flyers about your performance everywhere you go, ask your friends to spread the word, post something on twitter, that kinda stuff. Your performance doesn't even have to be that crazy or dramatic or highly gifted/talented or life threatening. It just should to be something unique and would get people hyped, like "I WILL BREAKDANCE ON LEGOS" or "I WILL READ QUESTIONABLE FANFICTION OUT LOUD" or "I WILL DO A STANDUP COMEDY WHILE DRUNK" or "I WILL EAT A BUNCH OF FLAMING CHEETOS WHILE SINGING SWEET CAROLINE". It should take place where there's enough space for a lot of people(you never know), like a park center or a townsquare. Though remind yourself about the law while doing this, be careful, you don't wanna get yourself arrested do you? And also have respect for the people who live where you're performing, like doing a song concert at midnight wouldn't be very nice for the people trying to sleep. Now, during the performance, you should have some capability hyping people up. If your performance were to take some time(30+ minutes), you should have some way of keeping people entertained, like bringing in a volunteer every once in a while or a complete switchup of the concept in the middle of it(standup comedy while drunk to eating flaming cheetos while singing).
